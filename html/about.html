Hello! I am Maaviyah, a student at Riphah International University. I developed this portal to bridge the gap between complex academic grading policies and student-centric accessibility.

My mission was to deploy a transparent, high-accuracy utility that empowers fellow students to track their academic trajectory with confidence and precision.

Project Mission

This portal is built on the principles of accuracy, simplicity, and openness. Many students struggle with the university's specific 0.1 increment rule; this tool automates that logic to provide "Official-Grade" results instantly.

"Technology should simplify academic life, not complicate it."

Community & Transparency

The Riphahians portal is a Community-Driven Open Source Initiative. By making the source code public, we ensure total transparency in how your GPA is calculated and allow for rapid updates as university policies evolve.

Development & Contributions

We welcome students and developers to help maintain the integrity of this tool:

  • Audit & Report: Flag calculation discrepancies or logic bugs.
  • Feature Requests: Propose enhancements like PDF transcripts or Dark Mode.
  • Policy Updates: Assist in maintaining the criteria.html ledger.
